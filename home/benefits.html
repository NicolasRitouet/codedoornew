How it works

STEP 1

Decide if you want to be an online or offline tutor or both.

STEP 2

Spend 1h-2h per week with the student.
Students meet twice a week with tutors and daily use slack to connect.

STEP 3

Give people a future. Find new co-workers.

Who can be a tutor?
Eligible is every developer who wants to give people a future. Even if you are just a beginner. You will not bediveve how much you will learn by teaching others how to code. The easy way to find out if you want to join our tutor network is by simply trying it out. No strings attached, of course. Just contact us via email or join this slack channel.
